-= Soulful Ninja =-
Soulful ninja is a 2D sidescrolling action rpg game where the player controls Churo as he tries to save the world.

Gameplay:
Churo has two sides with him, the dark and the light. When he's in light, he gains elemental powers. When he's in the dark he gains a bunch of ninja moves.

-------------------------------
Controls:
- A -> move left
- D -> move right
- Spacebar -> jump
- A while beside a wall -> wall grab
- Spacebar while wall grab -> wall jump
- U -> basic attack
- I -> use selected skill
- O -> switch between skills
